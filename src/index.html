Number of parameters to be checked

In medicine, when a person undergoes a treatment, it is crucial to determine the number of different types of pills they consume daily to prevent potential side effects. Indeed, the various molecules contained in the pills may interact with each other, necessitating a thorough analysis of the impact of each. For instance, if a person takes three pills a day (pill A, pill B, and pill C), scientists must understand the behavior of each pill individually, including pill A alone, pill B alone, pill C alone, the combination of pills A and B, pills A and C, pills B and C, and the combination of pills A, B, and C. This entails analyzing a total of seven combinations before ensuring the person that the treatment is safe.
